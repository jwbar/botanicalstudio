About Us

We specialize in creating innovative, controlled grow lamps that are fine-tuned and custom-built to meet your specific setup needs. Our mission is to revolutionize indoor farming by developing advanced automation systems for climate control and irrigation, ensuring optimal growth conditions for your plants.

Our state-of-the-art monitoring software allows you to control and observe your grow setups remotely, providing you with peace of mind and the convenience of managing your farm from anywhere.

Based in Germany, we also offer expert consultation services to help you navigate the process of finding and establishing a cannabis club. Our team assists with founding documents and licensing, ensuring a smooth and compliant setup.

Join us in the future of farming with our cutting-edge solutions designed to maximize efficiency and yield in your indoor growing environment.
